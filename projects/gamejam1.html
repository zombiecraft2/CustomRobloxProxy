GameJam 1

ABOUT THE PROJECT :

This project was a 3 days long GameJam, I participated to it with two of my friends. We won in first position and got rewards!

The project's theme was about "disconnection"/"unplug" so we made a small story around the player's limbs that he can disconnect from its body.

The game name is "Where Are My Arms" and it's available on Roblox : Where Are My Arms
